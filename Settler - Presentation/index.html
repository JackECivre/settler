Who We Are

We are immigrants who experienced the struggle of settling to a new country.

Settler is a community driven platform that strives to help new immigrants adjust to their new home. We are a one stop shop where you can find all of the resources you need to be successful. Constantly seeking feedback from our community the platform will dynamically change to tailor the needs of our users.

What We Do

We gather together every tool you need to simplify your settling process.

Translation

Integrated with Google Translate, our platform will be able to be used by anyone coming from any country. We supports all languages.

Necessary Applications

You can downloads all the apps needed for you new country in order to make a seamless transition. Our goal is to make your life more convenient through technology.

Interactive Maps

Provided by Google, you are provided with an interactive map that has pre-placed important locations around you. This can be anywhere from Police Stations, Hospitals, Government Offices, or Community Centers.

Product Prices

We would like to help you save as much money as possible by letting you know how much things in your new area costs. We want to avoid you being taken advantage of and getting ripped off because merchants think you don't know any better.

Burocracy

The biggest struggle of them all. All of our government information is easily accessible, easy to read, and easy to understand. You can find the answer to most of your questions with us.

Creating Community

Through shared meals and activities of Meet-up we strive for our users to truly become a community and make genuine connections. Our goal is to bring people together.

Future Plans

We are here to help all countries and people of the world interact and integrate

Globalization

Our platform can be used by anyone anywhere. Given that all of our data and information in tables, it is easy to switch the outputted content to display data of any country to enter a new market.

Partnerships

Once we have established our community, we would like to open our services to partner with local businesses to drive are community towards them through sponsorship deals or targeted ads.

Memberships

Give additional features to community members who pay a small re-occurring monthly fee

Targeted Ads

Using user data through profile information, our fun quizzes, and recommendations based on our algorithm given a user's interests and preferences we can works with services like Google Banners to place target ads and revenue share.

Funding

Collect funding from Government Agencies like Misrad Haklita and The Jewish Agency to hire volunteers to expand our services and offer real life support to our community members.

Community Ambassadors

We would like to have an "ambassador" from every country our platform is running in to research and update data based on the latest news or changes in their country.
